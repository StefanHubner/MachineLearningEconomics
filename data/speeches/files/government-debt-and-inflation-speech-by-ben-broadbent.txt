Aggressive monetary easing by central banks, occurring as it has alongside very large government deficits,

be doing. Theory tells you that, to stabilise inflation, policy needs to lean against swings in demand and UK

sectors alike. Thatâ€™s how itâ€™s meant to work. The reduction in the cost of borrowing for the private sector
stimulates demand and inflation. The reduction in the cost of borrowing for the public sector is an additional
