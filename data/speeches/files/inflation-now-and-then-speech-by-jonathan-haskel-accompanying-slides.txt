‚Ä¢ The Factor Price Frontier
ÔÉò If energy prices rise
ÔÉò First round effects: Output prices rise: inflation in proportion to share of energy
ÔÉò Second round effects: if wages and capital returns stay the same, no further rise in inflation.

ÔÉò If productivity falls
